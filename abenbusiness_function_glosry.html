business function

Abbreviated as BF. Repository object that represents a self-contained (business) function within the scope of Switch Framework. Multiple business functions are pooled to form a Business Function Set. Technically, a business function is a pool of switches that are part of the same function. When a business function is switched on, the associated switches are switched on too. Business functions are created in transaction SFW2 and switched in transaction SFW5.
