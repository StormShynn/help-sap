ABAP CDS - DCL Statements

The data control language CDS DCL of the ABAP CDS follows the general CDS syntax rules and comprises the following statement for the definition of CDS roles:

  • DEFINE ROLE

Note

CDS DCL has its own editor in ADT, which is documented there.
