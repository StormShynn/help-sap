EXPORT DYNPRO

This statement is for internal use only.
It must not be used in application programs.

Syntax

EXPORT DYNPRO h f e m ID id.

Effect

Exports the dynpro with the name in the id. The dynpro information is taken from the structure h (dynpro header, dictionary structure D020S) and the internal tables f (field list, structure D021S), e (flow logic, structure D022S), and m (matchcode information, structure D023S). f, e, and m, can be specified as standard tables without secondary keys.
